gTRAP

This website gives you an understanding of the entire Gene to protein network. Enter Gene ID or Gene Name to fetch Transcription Factors, Alternative splicing, Proteins, Protein Modifications and Protein Variations.

Example

To get information about Transcription Factors, select to search either by GeneId/GeneName and check the box of Transcription Factors. Click submit to get all the Transcription factors linked to the Gene of Interest.
